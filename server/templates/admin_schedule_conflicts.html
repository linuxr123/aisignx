Schedule Conflicts

Schedules are evaluated per display: a schedule attached to a group also covers every display in that group's descendant tree. Two schedules "conflict" when they want to play on the same display at the same time of day on the same day. The schedule with the higher priority wins (ties broken by lower id); the other is shadowed. Use this page to spot accidental overlaps before they go live.
